Executive Metrics Glossary & Churn Logic

📊 KPI Definitions

  • Revenue at Risk (Churn): Total Predicted 2025 CLV of all customers categorized with High Churn Risk (P(churn) > 65%). These accounts represent immediate revenue exposure.
  • Upsell Value Potential: Expected additional spend if customers mismatching their calculated loyalty tier (e.g., Bronze but showing Gold-like behavior) are successfully targeted and upgraded.

🧠 Churn Classification Rules

  • Target (is_churned): Defined as a customer active in 2022/2023 who had no purchases in 2024.
  • Churn Risk Categories: Evaluated based on purchase recency, spend trend, email open rate, and mobile app usage:
    • Low Risk: P(churn) ≤ 35%
    • Medium Risk: 35% < P(churn) ≤ 65%
    • High Risk: P(churn) > 65%

🔍 Primary Prediction Features

The model computes predicted CLV using winsorized historical trends, digital engagement indices, and customer behavior features:

  • spend_2024 / monetary_total: Core historical annual baseline spending.
  • spend_trend_1y / spend_trend_2y: Winsorized year-over-year spend trajectories.
  • app_usage_score: Customer digital product loyalty index.
  • frequency / recency_days: Classic RFM purchasing behavior variables.
  • email_open_rate: Customer responsiveness to brand communications.
